Who I am

I am a computer software engineering student at the University of Alberta, currently in my second year and actively seeking opportunities to expand my professional experience. There are many reasons why I decided to pursue a degree and, hopefully, a career in computer engineering, but the most fascinating reason is that I will be immersed in the future. Armed with the knowledge to create history at my fingertips, I find it exhilarating. The journey ahead is filled with endless possibilities, and I am genuinely excited to learn. Transforming lines of code into elegant solutions makes me feel like I have superpowers. Although I acknowledge that this is just the beginning and there's still much to learn, I view it as a blessing rather than a daunting task.
